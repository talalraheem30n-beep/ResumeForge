Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about ResumeForge? We have compiled the most common inquiries here.

Is ResumeForge actually free?

Yes! ResumeForge is 100% free with no premium paywalls, no watermarks, and no mandatory registration. You get access to all templates, customization settings, and exports with no hidden costs.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. ResumeForge runs entirely on your browser as a static site. No resume details, contact coordinates, or uploaded photos are transmitted to any backend server. Everything is stored locally on your own machine via Local Storage.

Are the templates ATS-friendly?

Yes. The layouts are specifically structured so Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) can easily parse text components, contact blocks, headings, and lists. We also provide an integrated real-time ATS compliance helper to check your content details.

Can I import and export my drafts?

Yes, you can export your complete resume structure as a standard JSON file. You can load this JSON file back into ResumeForge at a later time to resume editing, edit on a different machine, or make revisions.
